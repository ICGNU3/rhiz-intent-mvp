## Key Design Decisions

### **Layout 1: Balanced Dashboard**
- **Pros**: Good overview, shows stats, cards, and actions
- **Cons**: Might feel cluttered, Intent Cards not prominent enough

### **Layout 2: Network Graph Focus**  
- **Pros**: Visual, interactive, shows relationships clearly
- **Cons**: Complex to implement, might overwhelm new users

### **Layout 3: Intent Cards Focus**
- **Pros**: Clear value proposition, actionable, easy to understand
- **Cons**: Less visual appeal, might feel static
